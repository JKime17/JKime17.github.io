Hi there! Here is a website of my 3 favorite sites that I find both interesting and amusing!

Activities Around the World

This first website highlights all of the fun and interesting places around America and the world. It gives a small description of the places advertised and says where they are and any information needed before you go. I have always been interested in travelling, and this is a very useful website that helps for if I need things to do.

Atlas Obscura
